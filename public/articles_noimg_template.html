Transport in SF

So there I went! I flew into SFO at around noon, it's only about an hour flight time from LAX (navigating the queues at LAX takes longer than the actual flight itself). After arriving, I took the BART (the local rapid transit system) to the city center which was very convenient. Tip: Get the digital version of the Clipper card on your Apple Wallet, so you don't need to buy a ticket every time! You then can just tap your phone and use most of the public transport in SF. The trip from the airport to Downtown SF took about an hour and was only around $10, definitely a lot cheaper than taking an Uber!
